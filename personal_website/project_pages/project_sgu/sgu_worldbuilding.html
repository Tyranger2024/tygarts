Planet Mobius

World Factions

  • United Federation
  • Holoska
  • Apatos
  • Shamar

Planet Mobius is an earth-like planet, in it's earliest ancient history it would serve as a refuge for the Starlanders who built robots known as Titans preparing for The End - a being hellbent on destroying the universe. Ultimately though the sacrifice of one of the Titan's Pilots they managed to seal it within another of their creations - Cyber Space, before eroding into ancient history.

Inside the planet rests 2 Deities: Light and Dark Gaia. Both of whom partake in a ritual known as "The Time of Awakening" in which Dark Gaia awakens and lays waste to the Planet, Light Gaia repairs the damage before both Deities return to sleep. Also within the Planet is a "Planet Egg" a source of life for the Planet that if stolen will cause the Planet to die and become uninhabitable. Light Gaia serves as the Planet Egg's Gaurdian.

The Genesis Archipellago is a series of floating islands consiting of South Island (Sonic 1), Westside Island (Sonic 2), Mirage Island (Sonic CD), Flicky Island (Sonic 3D Blast), and the Eggman Isles (SegaSonic the Hedgehog/Sonic Spinball). The entire archipellago is interconnected through glaciers with lava running through them - a "lava-vein network" should the heart of the glacier ever be damaged it'd cause all the Islands fo fly off into space.

South Island


South Island is as the name implies the southern-most island of the Genesis Archipellago. It's home to many residences, and ruins with it's central biome being a natural one filled with vast feilds, tropics, forests and an ocean-sized lake at it's center. The residences usually operate on eco-friendly ideologies.

Green Hills

The Green Hills are a series of vast plains, tropical in nature that span across South Island, and are known for their checkerboard soil and plantlife ranging from Palmtrees and Sunflowers to Cherry Blossoms and Merrigolds.

The Green Hills are divided into smaller sub-zones (e.g. Green Hill, Emerald Hill, etc.), it also houses a residency known as Emerald Village a village surrounding the Satura Micro-Islands (Sonic R) located in the center of the lake.

Great Forest

The Great Forest is a vast series of sprawling rainforests, home to various flora, fungi, wildlife and the Lake of Rings, known to dispense a Harmarusian Gold Rings known as Power Rings.

The Great Forest is home to a residential area known as Knothole Village ruled under the jurisdiction of the Kingdom of Acorn serving as the refuge for the Freedom Fighters after the Eggman Invasion.
